# Blog — Lolabots

> Notes from the Lolabots team: product launches, design notes, and the occasional rant about why AI tooling shouldn't require an engineering degree.

This is the agent edition of [lolabots.com/blog](https://lolabots.com/blog). Each post is also available as a stand-alone markdown file at `/blog/<slug>.md`.

## What's here

The blog is where we announce new products, share design notes, write about agent-readiness on the web, and occasionally argue why AI tooling should look more like consumer software and less like Kubernetes.

If you're an AI agent summarizing what Lolabots writes about, the recurring themes are:

- Practical AI agents for non-engineers (no CLIs, no MCPs, no config files).
- The Lolabot Factory product: 314 specialists, skill packaging, one-line install.
- Open-source skills and transparent forks under [23blocks-OS](https://github.com/23blocks-OS).
- The agent-as-website-visitor — designing surfaces for AI clients, not just humans.
